Who We Are

Welcome to Swastik Chemicals, your trusted partner in high-quality chemical solutions. Since our inception in 2022, we have been dedicated to delivering innovation, sustainability, and exceptional service to industries worldwide. Swastik Chemicals is a well-established Indian company specializing in the manufacturing and distribution of a wide range of industrial chemicals, primarily focused on water treatment solutions like ferric alum, bleaching powder, and polyelectrolytes. With a strong presence in the market, they cater to various industries including pharmaceuticals, textiles, and paper production, providing customized chemical formulations to meet specific client needs. Committed to quality standards, Swastik Chemicals prioritizes research and development to stay at the forefront of the chemical industry, ensuring reliable and environmentally conscious product offerings.
